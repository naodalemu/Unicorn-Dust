Netflix Booth - SSDCC22

Clients:
UNIT9, NetflixLocation: San Diego, CA

Location: San Diego, CA

Netflix Machine is an interactive booth designed for the 2022 San Diego Comic-Con. Powered by upcoming Netflix titles, the machine was composed of LED cubes suspended in air to create an eye-catching 3D animated digital canvas. When attendees activated the machine, the animations changed to simulate a digital capsule triggering a Rube Goldberg style effect. We created custom designs and the wrap-around animations that were the focal point of this gamified experience.
