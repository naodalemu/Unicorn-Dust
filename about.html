About Us

Unicorn Dust is a Los Angeles-based creative animation studio specializing in Large Format Animation for Experiential Marketing. With over thirty years of experience, we develop customized experiences for international brands and institutions. Utilizing our expertise in commercials, advertising, music videos, television, motion graphics, and animation, we create media integrated into different kinds of spaces, screens of all sizes, resolutions, and specs. Embracing the possibilities of the digital and the impact of the physical, we are the link between these two worlds. Our values are centered around creativity and collaboration, driving us to deliver exceptional service to our clients.
